Glitter Bug Handwashing Activity

How to Use
  1. Tap the squares on the hand to show where the “germs” are.
  2. Press Next Stage after each wash to see how clean your hand gets.
  3. Watch the graph change as you wash better!
  4. When you finish, write what you noticed in the reflection box.
  5. You can save or copy your reflection at the end.
